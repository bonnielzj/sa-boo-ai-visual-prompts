# Render Pack Scope

## 30-minute quick decision pack

Use in early concept or internal review.

Deliver:
- 2 overall views.
- 2 material/lighting closeups.
- 1 before/after pair if existing condition exists.
- 1 screenshot mood board page in Figma/PDF.

Quality bar:
- Correct camera and material intention.
- Not final photoreal.
- Clear disclaimer.

## 2-hour client meeting pack

Use for client choices and paid concept presentation.

Deliver:
- 4-6 stills.
- 2 before/after pairs.
- 1 lighting scene comparison: day / evening.
- 1 material option comparison: A / B.
- Optional 30-60 second walkthrough.

Quality bar:
- Clean model.
- Coherent lighting.
- No broken texture scale.
- All images named and arranged in proposal sequence.

## Half-day refined proposal pack

Use for formal proposal or case study.

Deliver:
- 8-12 stills.
- 3-5 before/after pairs.
- 1 short video 30-90 seconds.
- 1 panorama or interactive presentation if useful.
- Crops for Figma, PDF, Xiaohongshu, Douyin.

Quality bar:
- Material scale and reflection checked.
- Lighting scenes intentional.
- Camera story complete: establishing, experience, detail, comparison.
- Exported images packaged with shot log and QA checklist.

## Naming convention

```text
Project_ViewNo_ViewName_State_Tool_YYYYMMDD.ext
```

Examples:

```text
Villa01_01_LivingOverall_After_D5_20260622.png
Villa01_02_LivingOverall_Before_D5_20260622.png
Villa01_03_KitchenMaterial_OptionA_Enscape_20260622.png
```
