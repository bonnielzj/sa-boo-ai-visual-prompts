# Lighting Standard

## SA&BOO lighting story types

| Story | Use | Mood |
|---|---|---|
| Soft Morning | residential, calm luxury, natural material | gentle, airy, warm-neutral |
| Warm Evening | hospitality, living/dining, social scenes | intimate, cinematic, premium |
| Gallery Accent | brand/showroom/luxury material display | controlled, dramatic, sculptural |
| Commercial Bright | office/retail decision review | clear, neutral, functional |

## Three-layer lighting rule

1. **Ambient/base light**: daylight, sky, general indirect light.
2. **Task light**: kitchen counters, reading areas, vanity, work desk.
3. **Accent/decorative light**: wall washing, artwork, shelves, feature wall, pendant.

## Color temperature defaults

| Area | Recommended CCT |
|---|---:|
| Living / bedroom | 2700-3000K |
| Dining / hospitality | 2700-3000K |
| Kitchen task | 3000-3500K |
| Bathroom vanity | 3000-4000K depending on material/color |
| Office / commercial | 3500-4000K |
| Gallery/accent | match artwork/material; often 2700-3500K |

## Exposure rules

- Do not let windows burn out unless intentionally creating high-key atmosphere.
- Keep highlights on stone/metal/glass controlled.
- Use lower contrast for client decision images; higher contrast for social/editorial images.
- Match exposure across before/after pairs.

## Common mistakes

- Too many hidden lights creating unrealistic brightness.
- Decorative lights with no visible source or logic.
- All lights same color temperature.
- Over-blue daylight mixed with over-yellow interior lights.
- No shadow; space becomes flat and cheap.

## SA&BOO lighting checklist

- [ ] One clear lighting story selected.
- [ ] Main light direction supports spatial narrative.
- [ ] Task lighting supports actual use.
- [ ] Accent lighting highlights design value.
- [ ] Metal/stone reflections are controlled.
- [ ] Before/after uses identical lighting settings.
