# SA&BOO Liquid Silver Style

## SA&BOO visual-first hard rule

- Treat `sa-boo-visual-first-core` as the governing rule for SA&BOO work: build or cite a high-quality visual asset chain before writing long text whenever the task involves design learning, research, style, proposal, prompt, rendering, CAD/FF&E assets, or project review.
- Use `sa-boo-visual-asset-index` when assets must be cached, cited, tagged, linked, or searched.
- Reject low-quality assets: blurry, unreadable, over-compressed, unattributed, aesthetically weak, irrelevant, or legally unsafe. **宁缺毋滥。**
- Keep the method efficient: do not force irrelevant images into quick text-only answers; for substantial outputs, include asset IDs, thumbnails/paths, sources, rights status, and quality notes.
- Let text serve the visuals: summarize, compare, decide, and translate; do not replace visual evidence with pure prose.

Create prompts in SA&BOO's liquid silver metal language: chrome luxury + acid design + Y2K future + clean high-end composition.

## Style DNA

- Material: liquid silver, mercury texture, chrome, iridescent metal, polished metal, frosted translucent acrylic, pearl glow, laser reflection.
- Mood: futuristic, luxurious, mysterious, cold, sacred, surreal, fashion-forward.
- Color: silver white, black-silver, pale blue, violet-blue, pearl, neon edge, low-saturation background, occasional acid color contrast.
- Light: soft glow, diffused light, rim light, Tyndall effect, cinematic lighting, reflective highlights.
- Composition: clean background, central symmetry, large negative space, editorial poster, product close-up, high-end still life.
- Texture: metallic gloss, fluid growth, chromatic aberration, film grain, soft blur, high resolution, fine detail.

## Prompt recipe

Always include:

1. Subject: object/person/flower/logo/product.
2. Material transformation: translate subject into liquid silver / chrome / mercury.
3. Composition: center, symmetry, negative space, poster/KV/product shot.
4. Light and atmosphere: rim light, glow, diffusion, cinematic reflection.
5. Quality: high detail, luxury editorial, 8K/ultra high resolution if platform accepts.
6. Constraints: clean background, no extra text unless requested.

## English prompt skeleton

```text
{subject}, liquid silver metal aesthetic, mercury-like fluid chrome texture, iridescent reflections, acid design and Y2K futurism, futuristic luxury editorial visual, {composition}, {background}, soft diffused rim light, subtle chromatic aberration, clean negative space, premium fashion art direction, ultra-detailed reflective material, cinematic lighting, high-end poster quality.
```

## Variants

- Minimal luxury: reduce color, increase negative space, use pure white/black background.
- Acid cyber: add neon purple/blue/green edges, glitch, high contrast.
- Sacred surreal: add flowers, tears, veil, wings, ritual symmetry, dreamcore atmosphere.
- Product KV: add studio photography, shadow, close-up, premium packaging/product context.

## Negative constraints

Avoid messy background, low-quality plastic look, cheap glitter, random text, excessive elements, watermark, logo unless requested.
