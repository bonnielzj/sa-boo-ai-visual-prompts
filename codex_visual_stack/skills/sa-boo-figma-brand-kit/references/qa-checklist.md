# Figma Brand Kit QA Checklist

## Foundations

- Variables exist before components.
- Semantic variables are used for template fills/text where possible.
- Text styles are named and applied; no random font sizes in templates.
- Color contrast is readable for social covers and proposal body text.
- Export sizes are documented for A4, 16:9, 3:4, 9:16.

## Components

- Components use deterministic names.
- Instances, not detached copies, are used inside templates.
- Variants are limited and useful; avoid massive variant matrices.
- Component properties are clear: Title, Subtitle, Image, Theme, Platform.

## Brand expression

- SA&BOO tone: restrained luxury, Eastern-Western spatial narrative, material translation.
- Avoid generic Canva/template feeling.
- Keep negative space and grid discipline.
- High-impact pages can be expressive; body pages must be readable.

## Production

- Replace placeholder images before final export.
- Check logo safe area and minimum size.
- Confirm fonts are available or converted/embedded when exporting.
- Export PDF/PNG and visually inspect.
- Keep client/private project information out of reusable templates.
