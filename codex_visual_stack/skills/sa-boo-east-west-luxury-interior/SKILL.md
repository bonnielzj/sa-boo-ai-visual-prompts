# SA&BOO East-West Luxury Interior

## SA&BOO visual-first hard rule

- Treat `sa-boo-visual-first-core` as the governing rule for SA&BOO work: build or cite a high-quality visual asset chain before writing long text whenever the task involves design learning, research, style, proposal, prompt, rendering, CAD/FF&E assets, or project review.
- Use `sa-boo-visual-asset-index` when assets must be cached, cited, tagged, linked, or searched.
- Reject low-quality assets: blurry, unreadable, over-compressed, unattributed, aesthetically weak, irrelevant, or legally unsafe. **宁缺毋滥。**
- Keep the method efficient: do not force irrelevant images into quick text-only answers; for substantial outputs, include asset IDs, thumbnails/paths, sources, rights status, and quality notes.
- Let text serve the visuals: summarize, compare, decide, and translate; do not replace visual evidence with pure prose.

Write spatial design narratives that combine Eastern garden order, Western classical references, material poetics, commercial clarity, and low-key luxury.

## Tone

- Poetic but not empty.
- Premium but restrained.
- Cultural but not academic-heavy.
- Client-facing, proposal-ready, bilingual if needed.

## Core themes

- East-West fusion: Eastern gardening, Western classical order, modern urban rhythm.
- Time: history, future, memory, inheritance, innovation.
- Nature: mountains, water veins, greenery, flowers, light, air, seasons.
- Material: metal, stone, glass, wood, textile, gold, bronze, reflective surfaces.
- Experience: ceremony, openness, rhythm, comfort, humanism, work-life harmony.
- Business: brand value, corporate responsibility, cultural depth, user experience.

## Workflow

1. Identify project type: lobby, office, residence, retail, hospitality, showroom.
2. Extract inspiration: culture, nature, brand, site, user behavior.
3. Translate inspiration into space:
   - form / circulation / material / lighting / furniture / art installation.
4. Write in 3 layers:
   - Concept paragraph.
   - Scene narrative paragraph.
   - Functional/commercial value paragraph.
5. If bilingual, make English elegant and natural, not literal.

## Chinese paragraph skeleton

```text
本案以{主题}为核心线索，将{东方元素}与{西方/现代元素}进行并置与转译。设计从{自然/文化/品牌}中提取{视觉母题}，通过{材质、光影、结构、动线}建立空间秩序，使场域在理性功能之外，呈现出更具仪式感与艺术性的体验。
```

## English paragraph skeleton

```text
The design takes {theme} as its central narrative, translating {cultural/natural/brand inspiration} into a spatial language of {material, light, structure, circulation}. By balancing Eastern sensibility with contemporary order, the project creates an atmosphere of understated luxury, cultural depth, and refined everyday experience.
```

## Preferred phrases

- 流动的金色记忆
- 历史的厚度与未来的可能
- 光影如流水般在空间中轻盈起舞
- 低调奢华的场域气质
- 人、空间与自然的和谐统一
- 将工作转化为一种艺术化的生活体验
- classical order and contemporary rhythm
- understated luxury and cultural depth

## Avoid

Avoid overusing empty grand words without concrete design anchors. Every poetic sentence should connect to material, light, circulation, function, or user experience.
