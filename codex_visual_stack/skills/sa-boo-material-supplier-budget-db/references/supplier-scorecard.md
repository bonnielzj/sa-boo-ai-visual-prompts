## Criteria

| Score | Quality | Reliability | Service | Documentation |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 5 | consistent premium finish, low defect | always on time, proactive | fast, clear, solves problems | full specs, certs, warranty, photos |

| 1 | unacceptable | high risk | difficult | no reliable docs |

## What to record after each project

- Did samples match final delivery?
- Was delivery on time?
- Were defects resolved?
- Was invoice consistent with quote?
- Did installation require extra coordination?
- Would SA&BOO use again?

Supplier contact, discount, private pricing, and WeChat records are internal. Remove before client-facing exports unless approved.
