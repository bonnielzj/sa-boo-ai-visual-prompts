# Material Taxonomy

Use categories that match both design language and procurement reality.

## Primary categories

- Floor Finish: stone, tile, timber, engineered wood, carpet, microcement, vinyl.
- Wall Finish: paint, plaster, wallpaper, wallcovering, stone, veneer, metal, glass, acoustic panel.
- Ceiling Finish: paint, plaster, metal, wood, acoustic, stretch membrane.
- Stone / Slab: marble, granite, quartz, sintered stone, terrazzo.
- Wood / Veneer: solid wood, veneer, engineered board, laminate.
- Metal: stainless steel, bronze, brass, aluminum, chrome, powder-coated.
- Glass / Mirror: clear, tinted, fluted, laminated, mirror, smart glass.
- Textile / Soft: curtain, rug, upholstery, leather, wall fabric.
- Lighting: downlight, track, pendant, strip, wall lamp, decorative.
- Sanitary / Plumbing: basin, faucet, shower, toilet, drain, tub.
- Furniture: fixed, loose, custom, vintage, imported.
- Art / Styling: artwork, sculpture, plants, books, objects.
- Hardware: hinges, handles, slides, locks, tracks.
- Equipment / Appliance: kitchen, HVAC, AV, smart home.
